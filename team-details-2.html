Apostle Israel W. Abam

Senior Pastor GLA Abuja

An Author, Motivator and Conference speaker, Apostle Israel W. Abam is a highly respected and sought-after Minister in Abuja, Nigeria. Apostle Israel W. Abam is well known for his prophetic message of Purpose, Hope, Healing and Empowerment. He is widely regarded as a Father, Pastors’ Pastor, Mentor, Visionary leader, and Bridge-builder. His ability to transcend Age barriers, Race, Tribe and Creed with his unique approach to Ministry has seen to the forging of many strategic relationships between the Church and secular communities in Africa. He has a burning passion to see the rehabilitation of the Local Assembly to come into alignment with God’s original Blueprint and Mandate for the Ekklessia. His love for God, and His Kingdom is infectious. Apostle Israel W. Abam holds a Bachelors degree in Law and is called to the Nigerian bar and practiced as an Attorney in Nigeria, before his call into Ministry. He is the Senior Pastor of the GUIDING LIGHT ASSEMBLY, (Abuja Worship Center), which has its headquarters in Lagos. As a Kingdom driven man, he is an active member of the Pentecostal Fellowship of Nigeria, (PFN) Abuja Chapter, as well as a member of the International Coalition of Apostolic Leaders (ICAL). Apostle Israel Abam is married to Pastor (Mrs.) Dorcas Abam, a very supportive, inspiring and lovely woman of God.They are blessed with two boys, Israel (Jnr.) and David Abam.
